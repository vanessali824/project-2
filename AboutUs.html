Welcome to boba T . We are an innovative company selling easy to make at home boba kits so that every person can have boba whenever they want. Our at home boba kits are customizable, easily transportable, and simple to make whether you have made boba hundreds of times or never before. It's a great gift for friends, family and just for yourself to try.

There are many flavors and combination to choose from and you are guaranteed to find something you love! You can customize the flavor and the toppings to your preference! These kits can be used to cheer you up on a sad day, to celebrate a great day or just to have whenever you are craving boba. You can make it for your family and friends when they visit and on holidays.

Our founder, Vanessa, grew up drinking boba with her family and friends. She is constantly trying new places and flavors for new inspirations to add to our products. Everytime she travels, she always makes sure to stop at at least one boba place to try.
